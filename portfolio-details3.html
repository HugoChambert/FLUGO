MUMBLE: Discover New Music Through Language.

Mumble is a curated music discovery platform that helps users explore global sounds through language-based playlists. By selecting a language, users receive a dynamically generated playlist featuring tracks in that language, allowing for immersive cultural and linguistic exploration. Mumble leverages the power of the Spotify API to fetch and tailor music recommendations in real time.
