P[ART]AKING

DELIVERABLES

POSTER • SWEATER • PHOTOGRAPHY

P[ART]AKING blossomed from a personal passion project initiated in the summer of 2022. It was born out of my aspiration to create a collaborative environment where artists and designers could converge, exchanging innovative ideas and nurture each other's creative journeys. Among the several designs I had conceived during this period, "N°1" holds a special place, serving as a testament to the ongoing work and refinements that characterized this endeavor.

SANTÉ

DELIVERABLES

LOGO • PACKAGE • POSTER

SANTÉ marked my first ever work in package design. The inspiration sparked when I had observed the mundane appearance of various health supplement containers and recognized an opportunity to enhance their legibility and streamline their aesthetics. With this project, my objective was to craft a logo, package design, and advertisements that not only appealed to the eye but also compelled potential buyers to invest in the product.
